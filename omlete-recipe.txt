Ingredients Required

2 eggs
black pepper according to taste
table salt according to taste
oil 1 teaspoon
onion copped 3 teaspoon
green chilly copped half teaspoon

Procedure

Break two eggs in a bowl. Add onion and green chilly to it. Mix the ingredients well.
Heat a pan and spread oil on it. Pour mixture on pan, cook it from both sides.
Sprinkel black pepper and table salt to it.
Serve it hot.
